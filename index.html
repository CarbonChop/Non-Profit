Introduction

NO KID HUNGRY

Every day, kids across America come to school having not eaten a healthy meal - or having eaten nothing at all. The solution is breakfast served during the school day, preferably in the classroom before instruction begins.

School meals are at the heart of No Kid Hungry's work to end child hunger.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to ensure that every child in America has access to the food they need to grow up healthy and strong. We aim to provide every child with three healthy meals a day, 365 days a year by supporting school meal programs, community partnerships, and advocating for policies to end child hunger.

Call to Action

1 in 5 kids lives with hunger in America today. You can help fix that by donating. You can donate as little as 1 dollar.
