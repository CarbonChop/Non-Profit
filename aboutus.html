About Us

History

At No Kid Hungry, that's our promise.

No Kid Hungry is a national campaign that was launched in 2010 by Share Our Strength, a nonprofit aspiring to solve problems of hunger and poverty around the world. No Kid Hungry continues to be run Share Our Strength.

Ending child hunger is our primary focus, though Share Our Strength develops other campaigns and continues to invest them.

We value community engagement to find solutions, children having access to healthy food, addressing systemic issues that contribute to hunger, and supporting marginalized communities disproportionately affected by hunger.
